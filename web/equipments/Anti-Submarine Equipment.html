The formula used to compute the DPS values of the Depth Charges below is as follows:

[math] \begin{align} Damage per Second &= \frac{Damage per shot(Dmg) \times Number of Shots(Rnd)}{Reload(Rld)} \end{align} [/math]

Usage Notes:

1. All of the equipment below are equipped in the Auxiliary slots, but are listed separately as they involve entirely different stats from all other Auxiliaries.

2. All the values used do not factor in the Reload or ASW stats.

3. Each ship may only equip up to one Sonar and one Depth Charge. Only ASW-capable escort ships (Light Cruisers and Destroyers) may equip Sonars and Depth Charges.

4. ASW-capable escort ships launch a 28 base damage Depth Charge if not equipped with any actual Depth Charge equipment.

5. If multiple Sonars are equipped, the 'ping' will be determined by the fastest-pinging Sonar in the fleet, and the others will have no effect aside from their base stats. If the Sonar bearer sinks, the next fastest-pinging Sonar will determine the escort's 'ping'.

6. ASW aircraft are equipped in the Auxiliary slot of Light Carriers, but are upgraded using Aircraft plates.

7. ASW helicopter are equipped in the Auxiliary slot of Köln Retrofit.

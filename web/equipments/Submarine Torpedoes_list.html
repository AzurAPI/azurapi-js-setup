The formula used to compute the DPS values of the torpedoes below (against Light, Medium and Heavy armour) is as follows:

[math] \begin{align} \mathrm{Damage\ per\ Second} &= \dfrac{\mathrm{Damage\ per\ Torpedo(Dmg)} \times \mathrm{Number\ of\ Torpedoes(Rnd)} \times \mathrm{Armour\ Modifier}}{\mathrm{Reload}} \end{align} [/math]

Usage Notes:

1. The armour modifiers used for the torpedoes below are as follows.

Armour Modifiers Torpedo
Speed
Ammo Type Light Medium Heavy
Normal 80% 100% 130% 30
Magnetic 80% 100% 130% 20
Oxygen (Type 95) 80% 100% 130% 30 + accelerating

2. All the values used do not factor in the Reload or Torpedo stats.

  • On ships with lower base Torpedo, torpedoes with higher Torpedo stat but lower DPS may perform better than those with higher DPS but lower Torpedo stat.

3. Torpedoes have a small AoE radius, but these values in the table assume only one target is hit - therefore, they may perform better in practice than on paper.

4. Trajectories:

  • G7e torpedoes can home in on targets within a small cone in front of them (10° to each side to a distance of 14 units). However, they move 33% slower than standard.
  • Type 95 torpedoes start at the same speed as most torpedoes but accelerate as they travel.

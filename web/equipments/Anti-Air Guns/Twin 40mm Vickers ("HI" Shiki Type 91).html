Historical notes

Navweaps.

These were based on the 2-pounder Mark II, the predecessor to the Mark VIII "pom-pom". Japan imported about 500 of these guns from Britain during the interwar years. However, their poor muzzle velocity and range were severe drawbacks, and as World War II approached they were replaced by the 25mm Type 96.

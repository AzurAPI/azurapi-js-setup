The formula used to compute the DPS values of the AA guns below is as follows:

[math] \begin{align} \mathrm{Damage\ per\ Second} &= \dfrac{\mathrm{Damage\ per\ shot(Dmg)} \times \mathrm{Number\ of\ Shots(Rnd)}}{\mathrm{Reload(Rld)} + \mathrm{Absolute\ Cooldown}} \end{align} [/math]

Usage Notes:

1. The constants used for AA guns are as follows:

Absolute Cooldown = 0.5 seconds.

2. All the values used do not factor in the Reload or AA stats.

  • The Reload stat affects only Reload, and does not affect Absolute Cooldown, so relative rankings may change at higher Reload stats.
  • On ships with lower base AA, guns with higher AA stat but lower DPS may perform better than those with higher DPS but lower AA stat.
  • Please refer to the Combat page for the calculations of Damage and Reload.

3. Unlike other guns, the speed and range of AA attacks is determined by the average of all AA guns equipped across the fleet.

  • For a given set of AA guns, putting the fastest guns on the lowest-Reload ships and the slowest guns on the highest-Reload ships will maximize rate of fire. Generally, this means the slowest guns should go on DDs and CLs, and the fastest guns on BBs and CVs.
  • In contrast, the highest-damage guns should go on the ships with the highest AA stats, which will usually be CLs.
  • Of course, larger-caliber AA guns tend to do more damage and fire more slowly. So a rule of thumb is to put the biggest AA guns on the smallest ships, and the smallest AA guns on the biggest ships.

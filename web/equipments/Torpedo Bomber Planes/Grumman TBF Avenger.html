Historical Notes

Wikipedia

Larger and more powerful than the obsolete Douglas TBD Devastator, the TBF Avenger was introduced right as the United States entered WWII, with its combat debut occurring at the Battle of Midway. While its initial results were poor, increasing American skill at airgroup coordination allowed the Avenger to score many important kills. Between its folding wings, three-man crew, and large carrying capacity, the Avenger would be used for a wide variety of patrol and bombing duties both during and after the war, with a few water-bomber variants being used as late as 2010.

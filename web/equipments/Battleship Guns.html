The formula used to compute the DPS values of the guns below (against Light, Medium and Heavy armour) is as follows:

[math] \begin{align} \mathrm{Damage\ per\ Second} &= \dfrac{\mathrm{Damage\ per\ shot(Dmg)} \times \mathrm{Damage\ Coefficient(Coef)} \times \mathrm{Number\ of\ Shots(R)} \times \mathrm{Armor\ Modifier}}{\mathrm{Reload(Rld)}+\mathrm{Volley\ Time(VT)}} \end{align} [/math]

Usage Notes:

1. The constants used for BB/BC/BM guns are as follows:

  • Note that the damage values used for computation of Type 3 Shell DPS below use the Type 3 Shell modifiers only. In practice the calculations are significantly more complicated as the damage is a combination of shell DPS and fragment DPS depending on the relative position of its midair detonation to targets.

2. All the values used do not factor in the Reload or Firepower stats.

  • The Reload stat affects only Reload, and does not affect Volley Time or Absolute Cooldown, so relative rankings may change at higher Reload stats.
  • On ships with lower base Firepower, guns with higher Firepower stat but lower DPS may perform better than those with higher DPS but lower Firepower stat.

3. Sanshikidan guns have a 30% chance of setting any target they hit on fire, and HE guns have a 50% chance of setting any target they hit on fire. This will modify their DPS values in practice.

4. BB-type AP shells that hit their target have a chance to inflict an effect known as "Armor Break" on heavy armor. Enemies under this effect will take more damage from shelling.

5. HE shells travel slower than other ammunition types, and Sanshikidan shells explode in midair. AP shells travel the fastest of all ammunition types.

6. Manually aiming BB-type shelling on targets provides a +20% damage boost over base damage. The DPS of a LB3 battleship with a manually aimed first volley is thus 3.2x the stated DPS values on this page, while fully automatic BB usage is 3.0x the stated DPS values on this page. Exceptions: Jean Bart, Dunkerque, Gascogne, Georgia, Yamashiro Kai, Fusou Kai, Hyuuga Kai, Ise Kai, Abercrombie, Erebus, and Terror only shell twice even at LB3.

7. Shells fall randomly in a square region with each side length equal to the given spread, except for the Type 3 which has a 12° angular spread.

8. SHS (fired by Massachusetts) inflict Armor Break like AP shells, have a splash range of only 8, but also have a tighter spread of 9.

Notes
When equipped by U-556, increases the time she can stay in combat after surfacing by 4s. Increases her hunting range by 1.
When equipped by Bismarck, decreases the effect of the Out of Ammo debuff from 50% to 35% on the main fleet.

Additional Notes:
"We, U-556 (500 tons), hereby declare before Neptune, Lord over oceans, seas, lakes, rivers, brooks, ponds, and rivulets, that we will provide any desired assistance to our Big Brother, the battleship Bismarck (42,000 tons), at any place on the water, under water, on land, or in the air."
Bismarck and U-556 were constructed as neighbors in the ways of Blohm & Voss. Completed at the same time, the Commanding Officer of the Bismarck lent the ceremonial band to 556 (as she could not afford one). As thanks, the commander of U-556 drafted up a certificate of sponsorship. In it was written the pledge to protect the Bismarck. Unfortunately, fate was not so kind. On May 26th, U-556 was low on fuel and out of torpedoes, returning from a patrol. Spotting Ark Royal and Renown in the mist, she submerged. U-556 remained in the area and watched as the mighty German battleship was attacked and destroyed by the Royal Navy. U-556 failed to protect Bismarck, and remained at the location of her sinking awaiting rescue, forever reminded of the promise she failed to keep.
More information can be found on the wikipedia page for U-556.

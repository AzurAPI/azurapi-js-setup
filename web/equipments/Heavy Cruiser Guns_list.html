The formula used to compute the DPS values of the guns below (against Light, Medium and Heavy armour) is as follows:

[math] \begin{align} \mathrm{Damage\ per\ Second} &= \dfrac{\mathrm{Damage\ per\ shot(Dmg)} \times \mathrm{Damage\ Coefficient(Coef)} \times \mathrm{Number\ of\ Shots(R)} \times \mathrm{Armor\ Modifier}}{\mathrm{Reload(Rld)} + \mathrm{Volley\ Time(VT)} + \mathrm{Absolute\ Cooldown}} \end{align} [/math]

Usage Notes:

1. The constants used for CA guns are as follows:

Absolute Cooldown = 0.30 seconds.

※ The modifiers for Normal are estimated from empirical measurements and may not be accurate.

2. All the values used do not factor in the Reload or Firepower stats.

  • The Reload stat affects only Reload, and does not affect Volley Time or Absolute Cooldown, so relative rankings may change at higher Reload stats.
  • On ships with lower base Firepower, guns with higher Firepower stat but lower DPS may perform better than those with higher DPS but lower Firepower stat.

3. HE guns have a 8% chance of setting any target they hit on fire, and AP guns will hit up to 2 targets in a straight line. This will modify their DPS values in practice.

4. HE shells travel slower than other ammunition types.

5. Ship Specific Guns:

  • Triple 310mm: Large cruisers only.
  • Triple 283mm: Deutschland class and Large cruisers only.
  • Submarine-mounted Twin 203mm: Surcouf only.
